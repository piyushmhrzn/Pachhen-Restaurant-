Special Events & Private Parties

Catering and Events are opportunities for us to work more closely with our guests, occasions for us to share our dedication to celebration in exciting new ways.

The Big Room

Seats upto 60

This is the perfect room size for hosting big parties, wedding venues and company meetings. When you don't know how many people are going to attend at a given time, big room is the best option. While reservation can be done over the phone, we also accept services like Yelp Waitlist or Waitlist Me or eSewa to allow book online. If you go with a reservation-only approach, then you can precisely plan the layout of your dining room for the best experience.

Customers who make reservations don't have to wait in long lines or deal with unpredictable wait times. This means a happier guests. If you do choose to accept reservations, be careful to avoid overbooking. Always give yourself a buffer.

The Small Room

Seats upto 15

A perfect room size for hosting small parties, birthday parties, friends & relatives union. When you know how many people are going to attend at a given time, a small room is the best option. You can precisely plan the layout of your dining room for the best experience. The blend of a traditional reservation and a walk-in allows you to reserve a place on the waitlist on your way to the restaurant.

No matter how you choose to take reservations, have a designated person in charge of the process. We will make sure to make your day great, fun and memorable.

Call (977) 555-1234 to reserve a room for your reception or party.
